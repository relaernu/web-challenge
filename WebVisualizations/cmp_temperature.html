Latitude vs. Max Temperature



As expected, the weather becomes significantly warmer as one approaches the equator (0 Deg. Latitude). On the date the data collected, the northern hemisphere is much hotter than the southern hemisphere. And that's in line with our common sense: it's summer for northern hemisphere and winter for southern hemisphere in June.
